Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

VisionX ("we", "our", or "us") operates as a content management service that helps users automate posting to their TikTok accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize our app, we receive your TikTok user ID, display name, and avatar URL through TikTok's API.
  • Authentication Tokens: We store OAuth tokens to maintain your connection with TikTok and post content on your behalf.
  • Content Data: Videos and metadata you provide for posting to TikTok.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Upload and publish content to your authorized TikTok accounts
  • Maintain your session and authorization status

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Authentication tokens are stored securely and are only used for authorized API calls to TikTok.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with TikTok's API as necessary to provide our service functionality.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by revoking our app's access through your TikTok account settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Revoke authorization at any time through TikTok's settings

8. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with TikTok. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository at https://github.com/Frederik-Witte/Frederik-Witte.github.io
